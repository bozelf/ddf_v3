Bioinformatics Workshops

Whether you're new to bioinformatics or just tired of endless tutorials, this hands-on, 10–16 hour workshop gets you up to speed with real-life tools and data—fast, clear, and practical.

  • FASTQ, BAM, VCF – file formats demystified
  • QC with FastQC
  • Mapping with BWA/Bowtie2
  • Variant calling with samtools/bcftools
  • Visualisation with IGV

Learn the tools. Go deep. Stay in flow.
